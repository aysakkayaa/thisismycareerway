I've been a computer engineer for a while, but for the past year I haven't had a job.

At first I just kept applying. Sending CVs, waiting, getting rejected — or worse, hearing nothing at all. It started to feel like I was disappearing.

Then I realized something: I was spending all my energy trying to *look* employable, and none of it actually *becoming* better.

So I made a decision. Instead of hiding this period of my life, I'm going to document it. Every project I build, every article I read, every mistake I make — it's all going on this site.

## Why public?

Because I learn better when I write. And because maybe someone else is going through the same thing and needs to see that it's okay to be a work in progress.

## What to expect

- Project breakdowns with every step, resource, and dead end
- Honest writing — no fake confidence
- Comments open, always

This is my career way. Let's see where it goes.
